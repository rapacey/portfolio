a) Find the number of words in dictionary.txt that have at least 3 o's

grep -i "o.*o.*o" dictionary.txt | wc -l

b) Find the number of words that have exactly 3 o's and where all o's are separated by at least one non-o character Ex. Microbiology

grep -iE "^[^o]*o[^o]+o[^o]+o[^o]*$" dictionary.txt | wc -l

c) Find the thre most common last three letters for words in dictionary.txt that have two adjacent o's

grep -i "oo" | grep -E -o "...$" | sort | uniq -c | sort -rn | head -n 3
